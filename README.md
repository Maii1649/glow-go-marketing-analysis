# Glow & Go Salon Marketing Performance Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes marketing performance for Glow & Go Salon to understand:

- Which marketing channels generate more revenue
- Impact of seasons on sales
- Performance of customer types and services

## Data Description
The dataset includes:

- Date
- Season
- Channel
- Service Type
- Customer Type
- Ad Spend
- Conversions
- Revenue

## Key Metrics
- Total Revenue
- Total Ad Spend
- Total Conversions
- ROI

## Key Insights
- Returning customers generate higher revenue than new customers.
- Referral and Facebook channels perform better than paid ads.
- Summer and Fall show higher revenue.
- Some campaigns have ad spend with zero conversions, indicating inefficiency.

## Dashboard Summary
The Power BI dashboard visualizes:

- Revenue by Season
- Revenue by Channel
- Revenue by Service Type
- Comparison between Ad Spend and Revenue

![Dashboard](dashboard.png)

## Recommendations
- Focus more on referral marketing.
- Reduce spending on low-performing paid campaigns.
- Target returning customers with special offers during high-demand seasons.
